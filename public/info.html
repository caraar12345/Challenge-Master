Origins

Cyber Discovery is a goverment endorsed program that is designed to engage school children from years 10-13 into the world of cyber security. The Cyber Discovery programme is currently in the Cyberstart Assess stage. - More Info about CyberDiscovery and the Stages.
Due to the time between the first and second stage the Cyber Discovery Unofficial Discord was created and players joined creating their own challenges and even completing some additional ones from Cyber Discovery staff.
This is why Challenge Master was created, It is a central place to host a variety of challenges for anyone to test their skills. There is also a competitive factor when completing challenges because of a scoring system that allows points to be awarded when you submit the correct flag.

Rules

The rules are simple DO NOT CHEAT and if you find a way to cheat report it responsibly. Cheating in any form will result in a ban.
A non exclusive list of cheating methods:
  • Creating Multiple Accounts to gain score.
  • Creating challeges that do not actually lead to the given solution.
  • Telling people the accepted solution to a challenge, although hints are fine.
  • Brute forcing solutions.
Points are awarded as follows:
  • 10 Points are awarded to the first person to solve a challenge.
  • 5 Points are awarded to any user who reports a security vulnerbility in the website, see info. - I reserve the right to decide what counts as "a security vulnerbility".
  • 2 Points are awarded to users who solve a challenge but are not the first to do so.
  • 1 Point is awarded a user who created a challenge when it is solved for the first time.

    • I reserve the right to award and revoke score, however I will disclose when this happens.

Bug Bounty

As seen above one way of earning points is by disclosing security flaws in the website to me. This is to be done via discord DM's (@Dan_#4752) and you are not allowed to discuss the nature of the flaw until I tell you that it has been fixed. I will decide whether your security flaw is worthy of score on a case by case basis. However, I will not award points for showing me that the site can be DDOSed so please do not do this as if I go over a traffic limit the site will shutdown. Additonally, do not attempt to mess with the site in a manor that will affect the end users experience. Finally, as well as being rewarded with score your name will be put into the White Page of Fame where you will be regarded as a demi-god second to only the saviour himself.
